Your Personal AI Task Manager

Effortlessly manage your tasks with Talk Task, the innovative app that turns your spoken thoughts into organized tasks. With the power of AI, simply speak into your device's microphone to create tasks based on customizable categories you create. Whether it's scheduling a family dinner or jotting down a work assignment, Talk Task makes task management a breeze.

Voice-Activated Task Creation

No need to type out your tasks manually. Just speak into the mic, and Talk Task will convert your voice commands into actionable tasks, saving you time and effort.

Intelligent Categorization

Our AI-powered system categorizes your tasks automatically based on the types of categories you've set up. Whether it's personal, work-related, or leisure activities, Talk Task keeps everything organized for you.

Seamless Integration with Calendars

Easily keep track of your tasks by syncing them with your Google Calendar or device calendar. With just a tap, your tasks will be seamlessly added to your schedule, ensuring you never miss an important deadline or appointment.

Flexible Task Management

Take control of your tasks with customizable options. Mark tasks as important, schedule them for specific dates and times, mark them as finished when completed, or delete them as needed. Talk Task adapts to your workflow, making task management personalized and efficient.

User-Friendly Interface

With a clean and intuitive interface, Talk Task makes managing your tasks a pleasant experience. Easily navigate through your tasks, view important details, and make changes with just a few taps.
